About my Hobby

What is the point of learning?

The answer to this question is simple to me: The point of learning, personally, is to become someone that is better than who they were yesterday.

'Intellectual growth should commence at birth and cease only at death.' - Albert Einstein

This is a website dedicated to displaying an age-old hobby of mine: Learning. Ever since I was knee-high to a grasshopper, I have and will continue to be interested in learning something and expanding my knowledge in a variety of subjects and aspects of life. Whether it be STEM, art, people, myself, sales, etc., I have made it a personal mission in life to learn everything that I can about, well, everything. I have chosen the path of full-stack web development because I believe it will allow me to wear the most hats, which is useful in today's rapidly changing world. It also allows me to learn more about myself and what I like and dislike.

Astronomy

The stars. What more can I say? The idea that there is an untold number of stars and planets out there, "200 sextillions" of them!

There are about the same number of stars in the observable universe as there are sand grains in all of Earth's beaches, according to astronomy.com. It's humbling to know that I can never be the center of the world, let alone the universe.
